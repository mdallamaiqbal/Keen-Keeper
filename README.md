Overview

KeenKeeper is a personal relationship tracking app designed to help you stay intentional about your social connections. It helps you remember when you last interacted with friends, categorize relationships, and keep your network healthy and active.

Instead of letting important connections fade, KeenKeeper helps you notice who needs attention and when to reach out.
